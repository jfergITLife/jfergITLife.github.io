i started writing this because i felt like we've lost touch with the excitement. every day there's a new ai release. a new cutting-edge research paper. the next best thing. the thing you need to learn immediately or you'll be left behind. get this cert. buy this course. join this bootcamp. empty your account, guaranteed job.

what have we become.

who are you to prevent someone from becoming who they are meant to be. what societal status makes you qualified. we are all human. we are all capable of growth. we are all capable of change. life is not fair and that is ok. it is an unfortunate truth that we must accept. but what we do with the plate we are dealt with in life is the ultimate measure of our character.

you have a good life then why are you bringing others down? everyone has their own journey and their own pace. just because you know more now doesn't mean you won't fall behind.

we are here to be kind. to be helpful. to believe in those who just can't see it in themselves. that is why i am here. i am still that little boy who wanted to be an astronaut. i still have that fire lit in me. i hope i can inspire it in others.

marcus aurelius wrote meditations for himself. it was never meant to be published. it just was a private journal of a man trying to be better, in his own words, with no audience in mind. and yet here we are, two thousand years later, still reading it. i'm not marcus aurelius. but i learned something from him: the most honest writing is writing you'd do even if no one read it. that's what this place is.

welcome to my brain.
